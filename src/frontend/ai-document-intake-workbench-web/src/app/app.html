Document review workflow

Structured AI assistance with human final review

Intake sample documents, process deterministic structured output, surface backend validation flags, and keep the reviewer decision and audit trail visible.

  1. 1 Intake Persist a local sample document.
  2. 2 Mock AI Processing Classify and extract structured fields.
  3. 3 Validation Flags Backend rules identify review concerns.
  4. 4 Human Review Reviewer inspects evidence and edits fields.
  5. 5 Decision + Audit Final decision and history stay traceable.
Structured AI Output

Document type, extracted fields, confidence, routing, and rationale are presented as review evidence.

Backend Validation

Missing fields, low confidence, and inconsistent values are displayed as reviewer-facing flags.

Human Final Decision

The reviewer edits field values and chooses the final outcome from the detail screen.

Audit History

Workflow activity is shown as an event timeline for review and demo traceability.
